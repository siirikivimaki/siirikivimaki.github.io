Welcome to my academic website. I am a mathematician working in the field of set theory. 

<img src="assets/images/me.jpg" alt="Siiri Kivimäki" style="width:280px; float:right; margin-left:20px; margin-bottom:20px; border-radius:50%;">

I obtained my PhD in May 2026 from Université Paris Cité and University of Helsinki, under the supervision of Juliette Kennedy, Jouko Väänänen and Boban Veličković. In Fall 2026, I will start my postdoc as a Morrey Visiting Assistant Professor at UC Berkeley. My research interests in set theory include forcing and combinatorics of uncountable objects, universality problems and model theory of infinitary logics.
